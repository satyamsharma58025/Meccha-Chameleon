# Volume 8 — UI/UX & Accessibility

## 1. UX Goals

Make the loop understandable in under 10 seconds for first-time players.

## 2. UI Areas

- Match lobby
- Role assignment feedback
- Paint palette and tool picker
- Round timer and state banner
- End-of-round results

## 3. Accessibility

- Colorblind-safe palette modes
- Large touch targets
- Subtitle and sound cue alternatives
- Adjustable motion sensitivity
