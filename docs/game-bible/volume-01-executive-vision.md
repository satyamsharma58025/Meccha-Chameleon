# Volume 1 — Executive Vision & Product Requirements

## 1. Product Vision

Build an original multiplayer camouflage-and-deception game where players use paint, movement, and environmental awareness to survive as a hidden contestant or hunt as a seeker. The game should feel accessible on mobile first while supporting a future console and PC release.

## 2. Core Pillars

- Accessible social play on mobile
- Skill-based camouflage through paint, pose, and positioning
- Short, replayable rounds with clear win states
- Strong local and networked testability from the start

## 3. Player Goals

- Hide effectively by blending into surfaces
- Use movement and pose choices to stay unnoticed
- Tag or survive according to round rules
- Enjoy low-friction matchmaking and quick sessions

## 4. Functional Requirements

- Local and networked match start
- Round flow: lobby, role assignment, prep, hunt, results
- Paint sampling and application
- Tag and inspection interactions
- Mode selection for Classic, Overrun, Trade Places, Lone Wolf

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

- 60 FPS target on mid-range mobile devices
- Clear input responsiveness under touch and keyboard
- Deterministic round state where practical
- Extensible configuration for tuning and content expansion

## 6. Success Metrics

- Round completion rate above 95% in local test runs
- Average session length under 10 minutes
- Clear feedback for paint, tags, and state changes
