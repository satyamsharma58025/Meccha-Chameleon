# Volume 9 — Art, Audio & VFX Pipeline

## 1. Visual Direction

Favor a clean, readable, stylized look with strong silhouette clarity and environmental contrast.

## 2. Art Pipeline

- Modular environment kit
- Reusable surface materials for camouflage testing
- Simple character silhouette variants for pose and paint readability

## 3. Audio

- Round start/end cues
- Paint application feedback
- Tag and taunt sound cues
- Optional ambient environment audio

## 4. VFX

- Shimmer outline flashes
- Paint streaks
- Small environmental feedback effects
